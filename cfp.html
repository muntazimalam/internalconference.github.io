Submission Guidelines

  • ✔ Use the official template (LaTeX/Word). Length: 6-10 pages.
  • ✔ Write in clear English and include a 150-250 word abstract.
  • ✔ Double-blind review: Remove author names and affiliations from initial PDF.
  • ✔ Plagiarism checks are conducted. Mention AI tools if used.

Peer-Review Process

Rigorous double-blind review by the Technical Program Committee.

  • ✔ Evaluated by >2 independent reviewers.
  • ✔ Criteria: Originality, clarity, and relevance.

Publication

Accepted papers will be published with ISBN and DOI, and sent for indexing to Google Scholar and IEEE Xplore.

  • ✔ At least one author must register.
  • ✔ Best Paper awards will be announced.

FAQ

Can I submit an already published preprint?

✔ Yes, as long as it hasn't been published in a peer-reviewed journal or conference.

Are Industry case studies welcome?

✔ Absolutely, we encourage practical applications and case studies.
