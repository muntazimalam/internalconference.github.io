About the Conference

The Ganitara International Computing and AI Conference 2025 (IGCAI) serves as a premier global platform designed to bridge the gap between theoretical research and practical innovation. By bringing together a diverse community of researchers, academicians, and industry experts, the conference facilitates deep discussions on recent trends and breakthroughs in artificial intelligence. The event maintains rigorous academic standards through a double-blind peer-review process, ensuring that all published work meets high technical quality and originality criteria. Accepted papers will be featured in major indices like IEEE Xplore and Google Scholar, providing significant visibility for authors' contributions. Ultimately, IGCAI 2025 aims to foster long-term global collaborations that will actively shape the future of computing and allied interdisciplinary domains.
